## F. Practice & Reference

- **FAA written-test question banks** — free legacy question pools + many free practice
  apps/sites (search "A&P general practice test").
- **Type Certificate Data Sheets (TCDS)** — free on DRS [[E4]](https://drs.faa.gov/); the
  authoritative limits for any certified aircraft/engine/prop.
- **Manufacturer maintenance manuals** — often free for owners (e.g., older Cessna/Piper
  service manuals circulate freely; always verify currency).

## What to actually download first (minimal free starter kit)
1. **AMTH General, Airframe, Powerplant** PDFs (the whole A&P syllabus). [[E1]](https://www.faa.gov/regulations_policies/handbooks_manuals/aviation)
2. **AC 43.13-1B** (your hands-on shop bible). [[E3]](https://www.faa.gov/regulations_policies/advisory_circulars)
3. Bookmark **eCFR Parts 43 & 65** and **DRS** for ADs/TCDS. [[E2]](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-14) [[E4]](https://drs.faa.gov/)

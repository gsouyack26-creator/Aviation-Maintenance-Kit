## The single most important free download
**FAA Aviation Handbooks page** → the three AMTH volumes (General / Airframe / Powerplant):
https://www.faa.gov/regulations_policies/handbooks_manuals/aviation

## How this was gathered
- FAA.gov handbooks page fetched & confirmed to list FAA-H-8083-30B/31B/32B (2023).
- eCFR Parts 43, 65, 147 fetched & confirmed.
- MIT OCW, FAASTeam, DRS, EASA, Coursera, Alison, AgentJayZ URLs fetched & confirmed.
- Subject-matter content synthesized from the AMTH syllabus structure and standard A&P
  curriculum. Where a fact wasn't directly re-verified from a fetched page, it reflects
  well-established A&P knowledge — always confirm specifics against the current FAA
  handbook/manufacturer data before performing real maintenance.

> ⚠️ **Educational use only.** Real aircraft maintenance must follow current FAA
> regulations, approved data, and manufacturer instructions, and be performed/approved by
